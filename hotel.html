The Hotel

Hotel Greenview is located at suburbs of Pokhara, spreading over 20 acres of land. The cluster of tiny bungalows of the hotel that feature separate rooms, look similar to a small mountain village that keeps and maintains the harmony with nature and the surrounding. The resort has 30 carefully designed rooms that are decorated with modern amenities to ensure the maximum comfort and luxury. All rooms have spacious balcony offering a tempting view of the lakes, green hills and the Himalayas.

About 25 thousand tons of hand-carved stones were used to build its ten bungalows and 100 people worked for two years to carry out the stone works. The resort hosts 20 individual gardens that house seasonal flowers and trees, providing constant greenery throughout the year. Hotel Greenview is the ideal place for researchers, photographers, bird watchers, nature lovers and of course, the holiday makers.

Welcome to Hotel Greenview - the majestic beauty of the Himalayas and its diverse environment awaits your companionship.
