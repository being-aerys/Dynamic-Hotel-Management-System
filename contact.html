Hotel Greenview is 22 kilometers away from Pokhara domestic airport and it takes approximately 1 hour drive to reach.

Access from Airport:

After landing at Pokhara airport, you can easily get taxies on airport premises. Or our tariff includes transfers and you will be picked up by our 4WD Toyota Jeep (3 people can easily accommodate) or Toyota Hiace (10-13 people can easily accommodate).

Access from Lakeside:

If you are already in Pokhara and staying at any lakeside hotel, it will take around 1 hour & 15 minutes drive to reach our resort.

Access from Kathmandu:

If you are travelling from Kathmandu, it will take approximately 5 hours drive to reach our resort. While you driving from Kathmandu to Pokhara, you will reach place Kotree and after driving for 3 kilometers you need to take right turn from Power House. From the highway it is 10 kilometers and takes around 30 minutes drive to reach resort.
