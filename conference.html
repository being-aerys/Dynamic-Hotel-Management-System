The hotel boasts 3 conference halls taking into account the lack of good and faciliated conference halls.They range from a small conference room to a large and spacious one.The halls boast their own state-of-the-art projectors and are capable of functioning at any hour of the day.The halls are faciliated with ac's,heaters and proper lighting systems.The halls have already served various meetings and conferences including the conference of the then-foreign ministers of SAARC.The halls lie at the quietest corners of the premises that guarantee the privacy required.Separate guards are provided at the door to prevent any unauthorized person to enter the hall.
