Dining

Dining Service

The spacious dining room opening daily from 6 AM to 11PM serves mouthwatering local and international cuisines with impeccable services. With the restaurant amidst the beautiful surroundings, experience the extraordinary tastes of the cuisines crafted by our artistic chefs and enjoy the scenic beauty around you.

Candlelight Dinner

Candlelight dinner is our special service offering each evening meant for you and your special someone. Share the ultimate romantic experience at the hillside, in a garden or even at the terrace with a table specially decorated with seasonal floral arrangements and candle lights. The fully dedicated waiter will take care of all the details. Please note that very limited number of seatings are available each evening.
